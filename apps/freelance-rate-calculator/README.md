# 💼 Freelance Rate Calculator

> Work out the hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly rate you must charge to hit your target take-home income.

## What It Does

Freelancers often pick a rate by gut feel and end up underpaid once taxes, business expenses, unpaid weeks, and non-billable time are accounted for. This calculator works backwards from the income you actually want to keep: it adds your business expenses, grosses everything up for tax, divides by the billable hours you realistically work in a year, and applies a profit/buffer margin. The result is a clear set of rates plus a breakdown of where each dollar of revenue goes.

## How To Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (double-click works — no server needed).
2. Enter your desired take-home income (switch between per year and per month).
3. Add your annual business expenses, effective tax rate, billable hours per week, and weeks you actually work per year (leaving out vacation, holidays, and sick days).
4. Set a profit/buffer margin and your hours per billable day, then optionally change the currency symbol.
5. Read off the required hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, and project effective rates, and review the donut breakdown of taxes, expenses, take-home, and buffer. Everything recalculates live.

## Notes

- Runs entirely in the browser — no server, no install, no account.
- Everything lives in a single self-contained index.html.
- Utilization (billable vs. total hours worked) heavily affects the result, and the numbers are an estimate to guide pricing — not financial or tax advice.
