# 📈 Retirement Savings Estimator

> Project your retirement nest egg with compound growth and see what it's worth in today's dollars.

## What It Does

The Retirement Savings Estimator projects how your savings could grow between now and retirement using compound interest with regular contributions. It shows your nominal balance at retirement alongside its inflation-adjusted value (in today's dollars), breaks down how much you contributed versus how much came from growth, and estimates a sustainable annual withdrawal using an adjustable "safe" withdrawal rate. A hand-drawn chart visualizes contributions versus growth over time, and a scrollable year-by-year table details every step.

## How To Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser (double-click it — no server needed).
2. Enter your current age, retirement age, and current savings.
3. Choose monthly or annual contributions and type the amount you save.
4. Adjust the expected annual return, inflation, optional yearly contribution increase, and withdrawal rate.
5. Watch the result cards, growth chart, and year-by-year table update live as you change any value.
