# ✉️ Cover Letter Generator

> Generate a tailored, specific cover letter that maps your background to a job — right in your browser.

## What It Does

The Cover Letter Generator turns a job description and your resume highlights into a strong, non-generic cover letter. You choose the tone (professional, enthusiastic, concise, or friendly) and length (short, medium, or long), and it prompts your chosen AI model to write a letter that connects your real experience to the specific role and company — avoiding clichés and placeholder text. The result lands in an editable box so you can tweak it, then copy it or download it as a `.txt` file.

## How To Use

1. Open Settings and add your AI provider API key (Claude by default, or any OpenAI-compatible endpoint).
2. Fill in your name, the role/title, the company name, the job description, and your background/resume highlights.
3. Pick a tone and length, then click **Generate**.
4. Edit the letter in the text box as needed, then use **Copy** or **Download (.txt)** — or click **Regenerate** for a fresh take.

## Notes

- Runs entirely in the browser — no server of ours, no install, no account.
- Everything lives in a single self-contained index.html.
- Bring your own AI key: it is stored only in your browser, and your text is sent only to the provider you configure. A browser-stored key is visible to anyone with access to this device.
