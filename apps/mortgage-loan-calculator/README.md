# 🏡 Mortgage / Loan Calculator

> Offline, single-file mortgage and loan calculator with full amortization schedule and extra-payment savings.

## What It Does

Calculates your monthly payment using the standard amortization formula (and handles 0% interest-free loans correctly), then shows total interest, total of payments, and a complete amortization schedule you can view by month or by year. Enter a loan amount directly or derive it from a home price and down payment, fold in mortgage extras (property tax, insurance, PMI, HOA) for a true monthly total, and add an optional extra monthly payment to see exactly how much interest you save and how much sooner you pay off the loan. A small canvas chart shows principal vs interest vs remaining balance over the life of the loan.

## How To Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (double-click works — no server needed).
2. Choose your loan basis: enter a loan amount, or a home price plus a down payment (% or fixed amount).
3. Enter the annual interest rate and the term (in years or months), and optionally a start date.
4. Optionally add property tax, insurance, PMI, and HOA to see the full monthly payment, plus an extra monthly payment to see your savings.
5. Set your currency prefix if needed — everything recalculates live, and you can toggle the schedule between monthly and yearly.
