# ⌨️ Typing Speed Test

> Measure your typing speed and accuracy with a fast, offline, privacy-first WPM test.

## What It Does
Typing Speed Test shows you a passage built from a bank of common words or famous quotes and times how quickly and accurately you type it. As you type, each character is highlighted green when correct and red when wrong, with a live caret marking your current position. It tracks net WPM, raw WPM, accuracy, and errors in real time, supports both timed and fixed-word-count modes, and remembers your best score for each mode in your browser.

## How To Use
1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser (double-click works — no server needed).
2. Pick a mode: timed (15s / 30s / 60s) or a fixed word count (25 / 50), and choose Random words or Quotes.
3. Click the passage to focus it (on mobile this opens the on-screen keyboard) and start typing — the timer begins on your first keystroke.
4. Watch your live WPM, accuracy, and error count update as you go.
5. When time runs out or you finish the passage, the results panel appears with your final stats and best score.
6. Press <kbd>Tab</kbd> then <kbd>Enter</kbd>, or use the Restart / New passage buttons, to go again.

## Notes
- Runs entirely in the browser — no server, no install, no account.
- Everything lives in a single self-contained index.html.
- Best scores are saved locally in your browser.
