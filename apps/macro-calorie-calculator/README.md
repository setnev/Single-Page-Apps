# 🥗 Macro / Calorie Calculator

> A privacy-first, offline calculator for daily calories and macronutrient targets.

## What It Does

Estimates your daily energy needs and macro split based on your body details, activity level, and goal. It computes BMR using the Mifflin-St Jeor equation, scales it to TDEE with an activity factor, then applies a deficit or surplus for your goal. You pick a macro preset (balanced, high-protein, low-carb, keto) or set custom percentages, and it converts your target calories into grams of protein, carbs, and fat. It also reports BMI and a healthy-weight range, with a live donut chart of the split.

## How To Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern web browser.
2. Choose your unit system (metric or imperial) — values convert automatically.
3. Enter your sex, age, height, and weight, then pick an activity level and goal.
4. Select a macro preset or choose "Custom" to enter your own protein/carb/fat percentages (must sum to 100%).
5. Read your daily calorie target, macro grams, BMI, and healthy-weight range — everything updates live.

## Notes

- Runs entirely in the browser — no server, no install, no account.
- Everything lives in a single self-contained index.html.
